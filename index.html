Protestant Theology
Bachelor's programme

Do you ever get lost in the big questions about the relationship between humanity and God?

This three-year bachelor's programme will guide you through many of your questions, will hone the academic skills you need to find answers, and provide life-changing experiences in the heart of Europe.

Have you ever wondered how modern translations affect religious texts?

As part of this programme, you will learn to interpret key texts in the original Hebrew and Greek and go straight to the source of their meaning.

Are you fascinated by theology but wonder, "will it get me a job"?

This programme will prepare you for a range of future careers, including further academic research, post-bachelor's studies, religious service, educational, cultural, and social work — and any field where a humanities education is a valuable asset.

Language: English
Duration: 3 years (standard)
Form: Full-time, on-site
Tuition: 25000 CZK / year (ca 1060 €)

Why Study Here

Bridging centuries, creating the future

World-Class University

Not only is Charles University one of the first universities ever established in Europe (in 1348), but today it is the largest and highest-ranked university among the 24 countries of Central and Eastern Europe.

Living Reformation Heritage

You will not only learn about the history of religion but also live in one of the key locations that shaped the religious history of Europe. Prague boasts hundreds of beautifully preserved churches and is the hometown of Jan Hus, the grandfather of Protestantism.

Place to be

Prague is regularly listed among the most beautiful, safest, greenest, and most vibrant cities in the world. Thanks to i'ts great location and affordable public transportation, you can easily set off from Prague to explore other places in the Czech Republic and Europe!

Small, Personal Community

We base our work on a prestige study in small learning circles with a family-like collective. That means plenty of opportunities for discussion, as well as individual questions and answers. Collaborative learning and mentorship are at the core of faculty's culture.

Affordable studies

Tuition for our programmes is only about 1,000 Euros per year, and international students spend about 7,000 Euros per year for all other living costs (university accommodation is guaranteed). You don't need to be concerned; we will fully assist you with your admission.

Digital & Library Resources

Our facilities are located right in the centre of Prague, including one of the best theological libraries in Central Europe. Our students have the opportunity to participate in various international projects, including visits to universities abroad and archaeological excavations at historical sites.
